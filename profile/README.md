## Overview

OmniFocus Advanced is a powerful GTD-based task and project management application that serves professionals with complex, multi-faceted responsibilities who need a system capable of capturing, organizing, and surfacing the right work at the right time.

The project and task hierarchy organizes work at any level of complexity, from simple single-action lists through sequential and parallel multi-step projects with dependencies, due dates, and defer dates that reflect the real-world structure of your responsibilities.

Custom Perspectives allow you to create your own views of your task database based on any combination of project, tag, date, and flag filters, building the exact views you need to support your specific planning and review workflows.

The Review system guides you through a structured weekly review of every project, ensuring that nothing falls out of your awareness and that every project has a clear next action that keeps it moving forward.

For knowledge workers, executives, and professionals who manage genuinely complex workloads and need a task management system with the depth to match, OmniFocus Advanced is the GTD-based environment that has served the most demanding users for over a decade.

## Performance

OmniFocus balances performance with system resource usage intelligently. Operations are handled in the background, keeping your Mac responsive for everything else. Efficient memory management and smart caching reduce overhead during extended work sessions.

Apple Silicon acceleration is fully utilized on M-series Macs, delivering noticeably faster performance. On Intel machines, hardware acceleration is used wherever available.
